Victorian Great House Hierarchy

In this example we see the Bruin family. It consists of:

  • Mr. Conrad Bruin (master of the house)
  • Mrs. Edith Bruin (mistress of the house)
  • Miss Gwenyth Gallagher (sister to Edith)
  • Master Darius Bruin (eldest child)
  • Miss Abigail Bruin(second child)
  • Miss Josephine Bruin (third child)
  • Master Theodore Bruin (youngest child)

And of course, we have the servants of this great house:

Starting with the Upper Servants:

Mr. Clarkson, House Steward
Mr. Fergeson, the Butler
Mr. Swanson, the Valet
Mrs. Tischel, the Housekeeper
Mrs. Williams, the Ladies Maid
Mrs. Phillips, the Cook

And the Under Servants:

Issac, the Footman
Joseph, the Groom
Jane, the Kitchenmaid
Rosemary, the Housemaid
Madeline, the Scullery Maid
